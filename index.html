Mark Watney Tribute Page

Mark Watney takes in the views as the lone man on Mars.

  • 1994: Mark Watney is born in Chicago, Illinois
  • 2032: Mark was selected to be an astronaut with NASA Group 27.
  • July 6, 2035: The Ares III crew leave the Earth orbit for Mars in the Hermes.
  • November 7, 2035: The Ares III crew step foot on Mars.
  • November 25, 2035: A powerful sand storm hits Mars causing 5 out of 6 Ares III crew members to evacuate Mars, leaving Mark Watney stranded and presumed dead.
  • December 5, 2035: They were supposed to decommission The Hab, leave Mars and return to The Hermes via MAV. Mark must now find ways to survive on his own and notify Earth of his survival.
  • 2036: Ares III crew is notified of Mark's survival and performs the 'Rich Purnell Manoeuvre' with the Taiyang Shen to resupply for the trip to retrive Mark.
  • 2037: Mark leaves Mars and returns home.
